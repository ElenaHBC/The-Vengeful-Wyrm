 is an elf ranger, skilled with the bow and at home in the wilderness.

Description and Background:
This elf ranger is a swift and precise marksman with 13 hit points and an armor class of 10.
Wielding a long bow, they strike with an attack bonus of +3, dealing +2 damage with each arrow.
Their initiative bonus of +3 and athletics skill of +3 make them agile and quick in battle.
Raised in the deep forests, they have honed their skills in archery and survival, becoming a guardian of nature.
